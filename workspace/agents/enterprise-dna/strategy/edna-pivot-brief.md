# Enterprise DNA — Pivot Strategy Brief

**Status:** Draft v1 (2026-02-24)
**Current state:** Eroding training/software business
**Target state:** AI agentic services company

## The Situation

Enterprise DNA has been a data skills training platform since ~2017. The business model (courses, subscriptions, learning tools) is being disrupted by AI — people don't need training when AI can do the work. Revenue is eroding. Most remaining subscribers haven't actively chosen to stay.

**What's still valuable:**
- The brand (established since 2017, known in data/BI space)
- 44,705 active email subscribers
- Domain authority and SEO presence
- Existing infrastructure (Supabase, apps, team)
- Sam's deep expertise in AI tooling and agent systems

## The Pivot: Agentic Services

### Positioning

> **Enterprise DNA** — We design, build, and manage AI agent teams for your business.

Not generic AI consulting. Not chatbot builders. We implement *agentic AI* — autonomous AI workers that handle real business processes: sales, marketing, engineering, operations.

### Why This Works
1. **Massive demand, no supply** — Every business wants AI agents but nobody knows how to set them up
2. **Proof of concept exists** — This OpenClaw instance IS the demo. Agent org charts, CRM integration, automated workflows, sub-agent spawning
3. **Recurring revenue** — Ongoing management, not one-off projects
4. **The audience is right** — 44K data/analytics professionals = people who work at companies that need this
5. **Moat** — Every client implementation makes us better at the next one

### Service Tiers (Draft)

#### Tier 1: Agent Strategy Session — $2,500
- 2-hour workshop: map client's business processes to agent opportunities
- Deliverable: Agent org chart + implementation roadmap
- Goal: Land the first engagement, prove value, upsell to Tier 2

#### Tier 2: Agent Implementation — $10,000-25,000
- Set up OpenClaw (or similar) for the client
- Build 5-10 custom agents for their specific workflows
- Configure integrations (CRM, email, Slack, etc.)
- 2-week implementation + 2-week tuning
- Goal: Get agents running and delivering value

#### Tier 3: Managed Agent Operations — $3,000-5,000/month
- Ongoing management of client's AI agent workforce
- Monitor performance, update prompts, add new agents
- Monthly reporting on agent ROI
- Priority support
- Goal: Recurring revenue, long-term relationship

### Why Not Just "Consulting"
Traditional consulting: "Here's a PDF with recommendations"
This: "Here's a working AI team that's already doing the job"

The deliverable isn't advice — it's functioning agents. That's tangibly different and way more valuable.

## What Changes About Enterprise DNA

### Keep
- The brand and domain
- The email list (rename segments, don't nuke)
- The existing AI tools (Mentor, etc.) — become demos/internal tools

### Wind Down

- Learning paths / assessments
- Teachable/training platform investment

### Build New

- Case study / portfolio section
- Booking system for strategy sessions (Calendly or similar)
- Agent implementation playbook (internal)
