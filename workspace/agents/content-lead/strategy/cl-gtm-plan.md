# Content Lead — Go-To-Market Plan

**Status:** Draft v1 (2026-02-24)
**Goal:** First 100 active users
**Timeline:** 90 days

## Positioning

**One-liner:** AI-powered content creation platform that keeps everything on-brand.
**Hook:** One brand free. Create content across every channel — social, email, blog, video — with AI that actually knows your brand voice.
**Differentiator:** Brand-aware AI. Not just "generate content" — generate content that sounds like *you*.

## Pricing Model
- **Free:** 1 brand, full platform access (get people in, zero friction)
- **Paid:** Additional brands (agencies, multi-brand businesses)
- Target: marketers, solopreneurs, small agencies

## Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

### SEO & Content
- [ ] Audit contentlead.ai for SEO basics (meta tags, OG, sitemap, structured data)
- [ ] Create landing pages for key use cases: "AI social media generator", "AI email writer", "AI brand voice", "AI content calendar"
- [ ] Start a blog — 2 posts/week targeting long-tail keywords
- [ ] Suggested topics:
  - "How to maintain brand voice with AI content tools"
  - "Best AI tools for content creators in 2026"
  - "How to create a month of social media content in 30 minutes"
  - "AI content creation for agencies — managing multiple brands"

### Product-Led Growth
- [ ] Ensure onboarding is frictionless — signup to first content generated in < 2 minutes
- [ ] Add share/export features that watermark or credit Content Lead
- [ ] In-app prompts: "Invite a teammate" after first successful content piece
- [ ] Free tier should feel generous, not limited

### Social Presence
- [ ] Set up Content Lead accounts: Twitter/X, LinkedIn, maybe TikTok
- [ ] Use Content Lead itself to create all Content Lead social content (dog-fooding)
- [ ] Share tips, templates, before/after content examples
- [ ] Engage in content creator communities (not spamming — adding value)

## Phase 3: Amplify (Weeks 9-12)

### Partnerships & Integrations
- [ ] Reach out to complementary tools (scheduling tools, design tools) for co-marketing

### Paid Experiments (small budget)
- [ ] Google Ads on high-intent keywords ("AI content tool", "brand voice AI")

- [ ] Budget: test with $500-1000, measure CAC before scaling

### Referral Program
