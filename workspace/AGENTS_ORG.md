# Agent Org Charts

EDNA Claw sits at the top — orchestrator across both businesses.

**Key rule: Agent orgs are completely separate.** Each business has its own agents, its own leads, its own specialists. No crossover. They share a CRM database (filtered by `business` column) but the agent teams are independent.

If this gets unwieldy later, the database can be split — everything is already tagged by business so migration would be clean.

## Deployment Plan

### Phase 1 — Foundation (now)
Get orchestration patterns right with a few high-impact agents:

| Agent | Business | Why First |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| CL Prospector | Content Lead | Needs customers — finding them is job #1 |
| CL Outreach Writer | Content Lead | Found leads need personalised contact |
| Content (shared) | Both | Content drives everything — SEO, social, trust |
| SEO & Organic | Both | Compounding returns, start early |

### Phase 2 — Growth
Once Phase 1 patterns are proven, add sales pipeline, email marketing, customer success.

### Phase 3 — Scale
Full org charts operational. Add paid ads, community, advanced analytics.
